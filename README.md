# yixuan_portfolio

My first attempt at building a personal website from scratch with AI.

---

## What it is

A CV site which covers my work experience, education, and a bit about my ideas and thinkings.

This project is born in Google Stitch which was used to design the overall layout. Then Claude is introduced to fill the information and do some tweaks. For some small changes, I edited the HTML directly.

Live site: `https://yourusername.github.io/cv`

---

## What I learned

Although I am equipped with basic JavaScript and CSS knowledge and am very familiar with AI, there are still a lot of challenges I met during the process. AI is very capable of executing and landing ideas, while the key to a good project is far more than codes and techniques. It's crucial to have a blueprint of what you are going to build and ideas of making it more creative and refined. It's especially important to have your own idea and taste in the AI world which cannot be replaced (too fast).

To achieve better outputs, it's important to have good prompts. From forming the website to optimising layouts and functions, every step requires accurate instructions and clear feedback to save time and tokens.
